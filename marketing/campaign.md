- Actively searching for apartments in Prague

- Realtors, relocation agencies, property investors
- Need to show clients why one district over another
- Pain: "I need data to back my recommendations"

## Content Pillars (5 types)

### 1. "The Truth About..." — Shocking District Facts
Single-stat reveals that make people stop scrolling.
- "Praha 4 has noise levels 20% above the city average. At night."
- "The average waitlist for kindergarten in Praha 5 is 2 years."
- "Praha 3 has 40% more crime than Praha 6. Same rent."
- "Praha 7: the most expats. Praha 2: the highest rent. Praha 9: the fastest-growing."

**Format:** Bold text overlay on dark green/moody Prague photo. 5-7 second Reel or static carousel.

### 2. "District vs District" — Comparison Content
Side-by-side matchups that people tag friends in.
- "Praha 5 vs Praha 6: Which one is actually better for families?"
- "Praha 3 vs Praha 7: The expat debate, settled with data"
- "Praha 2 vs Praha 1: Is double the rent worth it?"

**Format:** Split-screen carousel (4-6 slides), each slide comparing one metric. Final slide: "Full comparison in your report."

### 3. "What's Actually In The Report" — Product Showcase
Show the breadth and depth without revealing actual data.
- "37 categories. Here's what we analyze for your district."
- "Safety. Schools. Noise. Transit. Rent. Livability Score. And 31 more."
- Video walkthrough: "Here's what 799 Kč gets you" (scrolling through category list)

**Format:** Carousel listing categories with icons, or short screen-recording-style Reel.

### 4. "Move Mistakes" — Pain-Point Stories
Real (or realistic) horror stories from people who didn't research.
- "I signed a 2-year lease in Praha 4. The noise is killing me. I can't break the contract."
- "We moved for the kindergarten. It has a 2-year waitlist. Nobody told us."
- "My commute is 55 minutes each way. The flat looked so nice on the photos."

**Format:** Text-on-screen Reel with dramatic music. End with: "799 Kč would have prevented this."

### 5. "Expert Insight" — Authority Content
Position Move Prague as the premium, expert-led service.
- "Why every report is reviewed by a local analyst"
- "The 5 things expats always get wrong about Prague districts"
- "How we verify data across 37 categories (without boring you)"
- "What our analysts found surprising about Praha 9 this month"

**Format:** Talking-head style or text carousel. Builds trust and justifies premium pricing.

**Photography:** Moody Prague architecture shots — bridges, rooftops, streets at golden hour or dusk. Never touristy. The Prague locals know.

**Carousels:** Dark card backgrounds (#1a2118 or #2a3128), green accent highlights, clean data presentation. Think: "intelligence briefing" aesthetic.

## Key Messaging Do's and Don'ts

**DO say:**
- "Expert-reviewed"
- "Local analyst"
- "Verified data"
- "37 categories"
- "Delivered in 24 hours"
- "Know before you move"

**DON'T say:**
- "API" / "automated" / "real-time"
- "Government data" / "public data"
- "Free" (except for the free preview)
- "5 minutes" / "instant"
- "AI-generated" / "algorithm"
- Anything that makes it sound like a cheap automated tool

The product is a **premium research service** that happens to use technology. Position it like a law firm or consulting service that delivers expertise — not like a SaaS product.
